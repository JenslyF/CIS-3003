University of Central Florida

The University of Central Florida, or UCF is a public university located in Orlando, Florida. UCF has more than 210 degree programs accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges (SACSCOC). We offer many opportunities for our students to thrive both personally and profesionally. Within our tuition costs we offer many perks for students. Some of these perks include the Recreation and Wellness Center (RWC) where we offer access to our gym equipment, group exercises, swimming classes, and much more. We also provide the Student Academic Resource Center (SARC). SARC helps students succeed in classes by offering tutoring in a wide range of topics and majors. In addition, we have many organizations that student can join and meet like-minded people. To continue, we provide Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) free of charge for our students. Moreover, UCF has its own police deparment and K-9 unit. They focus on the safety of the students as well as the whole UCF community. To finalize, by being a UCF student you get your money's worth not only in education but in resources that help you succeed and improve your well-being.
